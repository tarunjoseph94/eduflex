Overview

Eduflex constantly looks out for partners to help its clients around the globe build a better education experience. Through the "Eduflex Partner Connect Program" , we work together to engage more schools and learners, take education mobile and inspire greater outcomes. Join the "Partner Connect program" and become part of an innovative community with a passion for using technology to transform teaching and learning. Within this community, developers from schools, districts, colleges, universities, associations, government organizations, and companies are developing software applications to engage more students in exciting new ways and keep them connected and collaborating together.
